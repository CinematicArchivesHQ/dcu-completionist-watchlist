# Hall of Justice Archives — Catalog Policy

## Included

- DC theatrical and television films
- Live-action and animated television/streaming series, tracked at episode level
- DC Universe Animated Original Movies
- Connected animated universes including DCAU, DCAMU, and Tomorrowverse
- Golden Age theatrical serials
- LEGO DC, DC Super Hero Girls, Teen Titans, and other clearly branded DC animation
- Selected DC-published imprint adaptations where DC affiliation is central and recognizable

## Organization

Release order is the default global view. Chronological order is a curated alternate view and is most meaningful inside a selected continuity. Universe, format, year, and story-group filters do not duplicate catalog records.

## Upcoming titles

Confirmed but unreleased projects appear in the Upcoming area and do not affect progress percentages. Placeholder dates are explicitly labeled when only a release year is known.

## Excluded

Rumored, canceled, and unconfirmed projects are excluded. Behind-the-scenes documentaries, promotional clips, commercials, and adaptations with only a remote historical publishing connection are outside the default archive.

## Stable IDs

Progress is keyed to permanent slugs derived from the production and season/episode identity. Titles can be corrected without intentionally changing existing IDs.
